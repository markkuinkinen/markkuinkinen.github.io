Dank Sole


Dank sole is my first look into creating a game with Unreal engine which I created alongside Karin Domagalska. It was made to complete the "Portfolio project - 15 ECTS" course at Metropolia University of applied sciences. Although the gameplay can feel a little clunky at times, I had fun using Unreal to create it and I'm proud of what was achieved. A lot about the engine was learnt in the process of its creation, especially regarding blueprints.
